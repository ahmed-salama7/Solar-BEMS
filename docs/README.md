The thesis is the authoritative reference for the **final as-built system**. It includes the
complete component justification, the P&O MPPT and CC/CV theory, the full Arduino firmware
listing (Appendix C), the pin-level schematic description (Appendix A), the firmware control
flowchart (Appendix B), and bench-validation results across 10 operating states.

> **Citation:** Ahmed Salama, *Solar Panel-Based Battery Energy Management System (Solar BEMS)*,
> B.Sc. Dissertation, Istanbul Arel University, Faculty of Engineering and Architecture,
> Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Istanbul, June 2026. Supervisor: Dr. Oben Dağ.
